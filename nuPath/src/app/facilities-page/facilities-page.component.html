Recreation Center

On the Pei Campus is our Recreation Center, which houses various outdoor facilities such as a 25-meter pool, hot tub, indoor wood-floored racquetball court, two wood-floored dance and exercise rooms, lighted basketball court, two lighted tennis courts, multipurpose playing field and softball diamond, and a fitness path.
Indoor facilities include strength-training and cardiovascular equipment (CYBEX Arc Trainers, OCTANE Ellipticals, TRUE Treadmills, a Concept 2 Rowing Machine, and Schwinn stationary bikes), CYBEX selectorized weight machines, dumbbells, barbells, and various other workout equipment.

On the Bayfront Campus is our Waterfront Program and Boathouse, where you can check out kayaks, paddleboards, and sail boats. Free sailing and kayaking lessons are available through the Sailing Club.
